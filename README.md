### 🛠️ My Projects

**♟️ [Adaptive Chess AI](https://chess-ai-2-35h2.onrender.com/)** — *Orangy Design, Dec 2025 – May 2026*
A full-stack chess training platform featuring a React chessboard, Flask API, MongoDB-backed player profiles, PGN replay/export, and a locally-hosted Transformer model fine-tuned on Vishy Anand's game style. Includes adjustable AI difficulty and evolving playstyle analytics (ELO, aggression, king safety, endgame accuracy).
`Python` `PyTorch` `Transformers` `React` `Flask` `MongoDB`

**🤖 [AI Agent-based HR Automation System](https://github.com/raak-16/cubeaisol-n8nresume)** — *Cube AI Solutions, Jul 2025 – Sep 2025*
Built an AI-based HR automation system for resume screening, scoring, and automated interview scheduling, with bulk resume handling via a RAG chatbot and LinkedIn profile extraction using Selenium.
`Node.js` `Express.js` `MongoDB` `n8n` `Google Gemini` `Selenium`

**🚀 [PSG Team Aurora – Rover Navigation System](https://psgteamaurora.in/)** — *Oct 2024 – Present*
Developed and optimized rover navigation and autonomous operation software using ROS as part of a multidisciplinary robotics team — finalist at the **International Rover Challenge 2025** (Space Robotics Society, Goa) and **11th place** at the ERC Remote Challenge 2025 (European Space Foundation).
`ROS` `Nav2` `Gazebo` `YOLO` `Arduino` `ESP32`

**🧠 [Fine-Tuned Mistral-7B for Philosophical Dialogue](https://huggingface.co/raak-16/philosophy_model)** — *Feb 2025*
Used Unsloth and LoRA adapters to cut VRAM usage by 30% while improving conversational depth, trained on a custom philosophical Q&A dataset. 100+ downloads on Hugging Face.
`Python` `Unsloth` `LoRA` `LLMs`

**📅 [Events Recommendation System](https://github.com/raak-16/recommendation_system)** — *Oct 2024 – Nov 2024*
